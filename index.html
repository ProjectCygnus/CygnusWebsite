About Our Project

      From the very beginning, Cygnus was developed, created and designed with the vision of what should a cryptocurrency optimally provide to it's miners, traders, users and the overall community. Our conclusion is that the key points are decentralization, egalitarian distribution, network stability, ease of access and use. Following this path we created Cygnus and the POW/POS hybrid block generation method was selected for blockchain security and stability.
      Besides the technical development our main purpose is to orientate the whole distribution and development process towards community. We would like to keep the crowd up to date and give them the opportunity to contribute and influence the pathway which Cygnus will follow in the future, therefore now we are launching pretty much a basic blockchain, to distribute initially the coins, further development phases will be decided in our monthly consensus polls. The consensus polls will begin at the 1st of every month having a 3 week submission phase and a 1 week voting phase. The first consensus poll will start at 1st March 2016.
     Taking these facts in consideration, we planned the reward structure to be fair for everyone. This means that, we set up the Proof-of-Work block rewards in such a way that don’t disadvantages or advantages any miner. We achieved this by setting up high reward blocks, which aims the early miners, early POOL miners and even the latecomers.
      To create a totally egalitarian distribution structure we will give the chance to people, who don’t possess any high-end GPUs or any other mining equipment, to get involved in our project. For these users we prepared an airdrop/giveaway and numerous bounties which will help to get more people involved in Cygnus.

Consensus Polls

     Once a successful launch occurred, and we are assured that the PoW system works without any problem, our first IMPORTANT checkpoint will be the first Consensus Poll. As we've mentioned before, every poll will have a 3 week long submission phase which will be followed by a 1 week voting period.
         During the submission phase users will be able to submit their proposals for 3 different categories. The first category will be for Technical Development questions. Here will come all the topics which may be related with Cygnus technical development and adding or changing key features. The second category will cover all the PR related topics, such as ease of communication, user friendly interface, targeting new markets, etc. The third topic will cover all the suggestions for poll which are not covered in the two categories enumerated before.
         For each poll there will be selected the 3 most requested topics, one for each category. Once the final questions are selected, the voting period will start. At each Poll there will be multiple choices. Consensus means majority wins disregarding the number of voting options. Once a voting period ends, and we have the final results, the chosen options will be implemented in Cygnus Roadmap, and a new poll submission phase will start.
         The first submission phase will start on 1st of March, 2016. This will last until 23rd of March, when the 3 most requested topics will be listed for voting.

Airdrop

      One of our main goals is to make the distribution of Cygnus as fair as possible. This is the idea behind our decision to set a premine of ~8.25%. As we've mentioned earlier ~44% of the premine (949.920 CYG ) was redirected for bounties and rewards. The second part of the premine( approx. 4.58% of the total supply, 55.556% of the premine), 1.187.400 CYG will be distributed free between bitcointalk.org users. This fund will be held in an Airdrop wallet and equally distributed between the users who submitted a valid request, and was accepted after verification. A total of 1000 users will be accepted for the airdrop, which means that, every accepted user will get 1187.4 CYG.
     The Airdrop period will last for 2 weeks. In this 2 week period we will continuously verify the requests. Users who would like to take part in the distribution must send a PM to cygnus.dev user at bitcointalk.org
